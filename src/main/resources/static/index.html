How it works

Report in three simple steps — fast, trackable and effective.

1. Report

Fill a short form with details and optionally add a photo or location. You’ll get a tracking ID immediately.

2. Track

Use your tracking ID to check status — public and transparent updates are recorded by admins.

3. Resolve

Once verified, authorities/admins act and update status — resolved issues are documented publicly.

About Report Local Issues

We are a grassroots project that helps citizens report civic problems quickly and transparently. Our mission is to close the loop between citizens and local authorities — making neighbourhoods safer and cleaner.

  • • Easy reporting with optional photo & location
  • • Transparent tracking with unique IDs
  • • Lightweight — no signup required
